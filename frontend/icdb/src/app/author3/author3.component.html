Dan Slott

Date of birth: July 3, 1967

American comic book writer, who is the current writer on Marvel Comics' Tony Stark: Iron Man and Fantastic Four. He is best known for his long run on The Amazing Spider-Man, as well as Arkham Asylum: Living Hell, She-Hulk, The Superior Spider-Man, The Mighty Avengers
